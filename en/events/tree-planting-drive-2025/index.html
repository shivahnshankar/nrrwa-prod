Tree Planting Drive 2025

üìÖ 15 September 2025 üè∑Ô∏è Environment, Community Service, Green Initiative

We are organizing our Annual Tree Planting Drive 2025 to increase green cover in our locality and contribute to environmental conservation!

Event Details

Date: October 12, 2025 Time: 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM Venue: Starting from Namma Vana, Roopanagara

Program Highlights

Plantation Activity

  • Plant 500+ saplings across the community
  • Native tree species including neem, mango, and banyan
  • Guided by expert horticulturists
  • Hands-on learning about tree care

Environmental Workshop

  • Session on environmental conservation
  • Benefits of urban forestry
  • Tree maintenance tips for residents

Community Participation

  • Families can sponsor saplings
  • Children’s awareness program
  • Certificates for all participants

What to Bring

  • Comfortable clothes suitable for outdoor activity
  • Water bottle and hat
  • Gloves (if available)
  • Enthusiasm to make a difference!

Registration

Free for all participants. Please register in advance to help us plan for the number of saplings needed.
