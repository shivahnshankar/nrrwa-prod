Environment

Our environmental initiatives focus on creating a greener, cleaner, and more sustainable Roopanagara for current and future generations.

Key Initiatives

Tree Sapling Distribution

On World Environment day, each year since commencement, the Association has distributed Tree saplings to the needy free of cost. This initiative helps promote greenery in our community and encourages residents to participate in environmental conservation.

Park Plantation & Maintenance

Around Roopanagara there are 5 parks. We have been planting Flower and Fruit plants in these parks and maintaining them since inception. During 2024-25, we have planted 40 such saplings as replacement in the park.

Composting Awareness Program

Our continued effort to make environment more green - experts speak on how to convert kitchen waste to manure.

Our Impact

Through these initiatives, we've made significant progress in making Roopanagara greener and more sustainable.

Get Involved

Join us in our environmental initiatives! Contact us to learn how you can contribute to a greener Roopanagara.
