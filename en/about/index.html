About Us

Welcome to NRRWA

Namma Roopanagara Residents Welfare Association (NRRWA) is a community-driven organization dedicated to improving the quality of life for all residents of Roopanagara. Established over a decade ago, we have been working tirelessly to create a vibrant, safe, and sustainable neighborhood.

Our Story

NRRWA, is a movement among Roopanagara residents, started in January, 2021 and now active 5th year running. Association got Registered with Registrar of Co-op Societies on 09.09.2021. Roopanagara has 2,500 sites and about 800 houses. Association is presently having a membership of 300+ and number is growing.

What We Do

NRRWA works on multiple fronts to serve the community:

  • Infrastructure Development: We liaise with local authorities to improve roads, street lights, water supply, and other essential services.
  • Environmental Initiatives: Tree plantation drives, waste management programs, and awareness campaigns about sustainable living.
  • Community Events: Cultural festivals, sports activities, and social gatherings that strengthen community bonds.
  • Advocacy: Representing residents’ interests with government bodies and service providers.
  • Emergency Support: Coordinating help during emergencies and maintaining a network of support.

Get Involved

NRRWA is powered by volunteers and the participation of residents like you. Whether you want to join as a member, volunteer for events, or simply stay informed about community activities, we welcome your involvement.

Together, we can make Roopanagara an even better place to live!
