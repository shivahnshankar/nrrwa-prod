Purpose

This WhatsApp group is created to promote a Clean & Green Community in Roopanagar.

Guidelines are shared to ensure the group serves its purpose and maintains peace and harmony. These guidelines are not meant to restrict freedom, but to keep the group focused.

Informal & Formal Organizations within Roopanagar like NRRWA, WPR, DHBCS, DEST & even CESC use this forum for communication and coordinate with residents.


Topics covered by this group include:

  • Communication and coordination of eco-friendly and socially relevant programs, including calls for volunteers
  • Sharing useful information for residents (vendor contacts, hospitals, service providers, etc.)
  • Updates on local issues (power outages, garbage collection, water issues, etc.)
  • Tips and information on sustainable living practices

Guidelines

  • For detailed discussions on any topic, please take them directly to the concerned individual or organization outside the group.

Since this is a diverse community, we request members to avoid:

  • Political or religious or caste based forwards and discussions
  • Good morning/night messages, jokes, or unrelated content
  • Aggressive, argumentative, or personal attacks
  • Messages that cause disharmony

All Admins reserve the right to delete messages that do not align with the group's purpose.

Let’s work together for a Clean, Green, and Beautiful Roopanagar!
