Promoting healthy living and wellbeing for all residents through awareness programs, health camps, fitness activities, and preventive healthcare initiatives.

## Key Initiatives

### Health Check-up Camps
Regular health screening camps for early detection and prevention.

**Programs:**
- Quarterly general health check-up camps
- Blood pressure and diabetes screening
- Eye check-up camps
- Dental health camps
- Women's health screening
- Senior citizen health monitoring

### Fitness & Yoga
Promoting physical fitness through organized activities.

**Activities:**
- Daily morning yoga sessions
- Weekly fitness walks
- Zumba and aerobics classes
- Sports activities for all ages
- Cycling groups
- Outdoor exercise equipment maintenance

### Health Awareness
Education programs on various health topics.

**Topics Covered:**
- Seasonal disease prevention
- Nutrition and healthy eating
- Mental health awareness
- First aid training
- Hygiene and sanitation
- Lifestyle disease management
- Women's health issues
- Child health and vaccination

### Mental Health Support
Recognizing the importance of mental wellbeing.

**Services:**
- Stress management workshops
- Counseling service referrals
- Support groups
- Meditation and mindfulness sessions
- Community support network
- Awareness about mental health resources

### Emergency Preparedness
Ensuring community readiness for health emergencies.

**Initiatives:**
- First aid training programs
- Emergency contact database
- Medical resource mapping
- Disaster health response planning
- CPR training sessions
- Basic life support awareness

### Sports & Recreation
Encouraging active lifestyles through sports.

**Facilities & Programs:**
- Basketball court maintenance
- Badminton court access
- Children's sports coaching
- Community sports tournaments
- Walking/jogging tracks
- Outdoor fitness equipment
