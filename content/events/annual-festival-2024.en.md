We are thrilled to announce our Annual Cultural Festival 2024, a celebration of our rich heritage and community spirit!

## Event Details

**Date:** December 15, 2024  
**Time:** 5:00 PM - 9:00 PM  
**Venue:** Community Hall, Roopanagara

## Program Highlights

### Cultural Performances
- Classical dance performances
- Folk music presentations
- Traditional art demonstrations
- Children's talent showcase

### Food Stalls
Enjoy delicious local cuisine and traditional snacks from various food stalls.

### Community Activities
- Art and craft workshops
- Traditional games for children
- Photo booth with cultural themes
