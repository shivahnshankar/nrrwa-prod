We are excited to announce our Family Day Celebration 2025, a day dedicated to bringing families together for fun and bonding!

## Event Details

**Date:** December 15, 2025
**Time:** 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
**Venue:** Namma Vana, Roopanagara

## Program Highlights

### Family Activities
- Outdoor games and sports competitions
- Family quiz and treasure hunt
- Tug of war and relay races
- Children's play area with bouncy castles

### Entertainment
- Live music performances
- Magic show for children
- Face painting and balloon art
- DJ and dance floor

### Food & Refreshments
Enjoy delicious food from various stalls including snacks, meals, and desserts.
