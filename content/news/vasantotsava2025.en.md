Vasantotsava was conducted at Namma vana on 26.04.2025. It was a Karaoke music program of Kannada film songs. 13 singers from Roopanagar took part in the program and entertained an audience of about 300 for two hours with their melodious voices with Karaoke music of 20 popular Kannada film songs. 

The program was co-ordinated by Mr Sridhar Tantry, treasurer NRRWA. Program was anchored by Smt Uma Peshwa and Sri Badrinath. Following singers took part in the program.  
- Raksha Prabhu
- Sanvi Prabhu
- Vidya Satish
- Revathy 
- Ashwini B M
- Veena Srinath
- Sudesh
- Achyuth rao
- Sridhar Tantry
- Jayaraj
- Lakshminarayan
- Arjun.

There were a few food stalls setup as well and it was a big hit! It was wonderful to see the residents socialing and savouring snacks together amidst bonhomie and laughter.
