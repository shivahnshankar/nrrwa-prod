In our constant endeavour to serve the community better, we are proud to announce the launch of our official website. Obviously since you are viewing this on the new website, you already know how to access it :). The website is a platform to connect with the community, share information, and provide a space for residents to engage with each other.

The website launch was held on 05th December 2025 at 05:30 pm in the NRRWA office. The NRRWA Vice President Mr. Suryanarayana welcomed the chief guest Dr. R. Balasubramaniam on the dias. NRRWA Treasurer Mr. Sridhar Tantry introduced the chief guest. The chief guest then formally inaugurated the website. 

Mr. Shiva Shankar explained the salient features of the website. He also thanked the NRRWA members for their support and encouragement.

The Chief guest Mr. Balasubramaniam delivered a speech on the importance of the website and its role in connecting with the community. He also shared his experience of creating websites for the PMO and the challenges faced in the process. 

Mr. Suryanarayana delivered a presidential address on the importance of the website and its role in connecting with the community. 

Mr. Chandrashekhar delivered a vote of thanks to the Chief guest and the NRRWA members for their support and encouragement. The entire event was well attended by residents and members of NRRWA. Thanks to Mrs. Uma Peshwa for anchoring the event.
