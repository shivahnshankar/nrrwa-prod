The “Free Chess Camp for Children”, conducted by Mr. Srinivas and organized by NRRWA, was an engaging and educational program designed to introduce children to the fundamentals of chess. Srinivas provided hands-on guidance, teaching strategies that enhance concentration, logical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

The camp saw enthusiastic participation, with children actively practicing and enjoying the learning process. Parents appreciated the initiative for promoting cognitive development in a fun and structured environment. The event was a resounding success, reinforcing NRRWA’s commitment to meaningful community activities for young learners.
